Vision

Creating a dope-free environment and
promoting fair play in sports in India

Credible. Visible. Inclusive.

NADA India works independently, making what we do trustworthy. We work for all athletes, no matter their sport, affiliations, or specializations. For us, all athletes are the same. Our work is to promote fair play and the sporting ecosystem and can be accessed by all.

What We Do

NADA India implements the anti-doping programme in India, in line with the World Anti-doping Code 2021. It works in close collaboration with the sport ecosystem to create a dope-free sporting environment in the country.
